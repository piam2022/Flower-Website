Saint Barths

Beaches, boutiques, and food

Although Saint Barths is very hard to access, it is a wonderful place to visit. Saint Barths is known for beautiful beaches, boutiques, and delicious food. Saint Barths is home to many of the worlds most beautiful beaches. Gouverneur beach, Nikki beach, Eden Rock, St. Jean, Gustavia, and so many more. Saint Barths is also known for many cute boutiques like Poppy, Poupette, and Clic. They also have many yummy places to get food and some of my favorites are Black Ginger, L'Isola, Saline and Lorient, Pearl Beach, Nikki Beach, St. Jean restaurants and so many other places.
