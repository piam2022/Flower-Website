Hawaii

Beaches, Wildlife, and Adventures

Hawaii is a gorgeous place to visit. It is home to many beautiful beaches, wildlife, adventures and more. My top two favorite beaches in Hawaii are the green sand beach, and the mauna kea beach. Hawaii is home to many cute animals like mongooses, whales, dolphins, fish, and so many others. While you are visiting make sure to go on some fun adventures like ziplining, boogie boarding, scuba diving, snorkeling, surfing, and walk on the chocolate farms.
